关于我｜About me

你好！我是张慎卿，一个生于二十世纪末，成长于新疆的四川人，曾立志投身词讼案牍，川闽苏三地打过工，广东深圳创过业。2020年～2023年中国的大环境急转直下，身边的遭遇与过往的经历让我深感未来普通人生活大有鱼游沸鼎之势，而我也因此来到伊利诺伊卫斯理大学就读计算机科学专业。

Hello! I’m Shenqing Zhang, a Sichuan native who was born in the late 20th century and grew up in Xinjiang. I once aspired to pursue a career in law, working across Sichuan, Fujian, and Jiangsu, and even ventured into entrepreneurship in Shenzhen. However, the drastic shifts in China’s political atomsphere from 2020 to 2023, coupled with the experiences of those around me, led me to recognize the increasingly precarious future ordinary people face. As a result, I decided to study computer science at Illinois Wesleyan University.

平时我的爱好集中于烹饪，写作，阅读和爵士乐。在这个有时略显晦暗的世界里，我的热爱给了我足够慰藉自身的温暖与激情。我也愿意把我的快乐分享给阅读博客的你。

My hobbies primarily focus on cooking, writing, reading, and jazz music. In this sometimes gloomy world, these passions provide me with the warmth and passion needed to find comfort. I would also love to share my joy with you, the reader of this blog.

期待与你分享我在北美大陆上新的冒险。

I look forward to sharing my new adventures in North America with you.
