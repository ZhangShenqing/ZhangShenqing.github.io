关于我｜About me

你好！我是张慎卿，一个生于二十世纪末，成长于新疆的四川人，曾立志投身词讼案牍，川闽苏三地打过工，广东深圳创过业。2020年～2023年，身边的大环境急转直下，我深感未来普通人生活大有鱼游沸鼎之势，也因此来到伊利诺伊卫斯理大学就读计算机科学专业。

Hello, I am Zhang Shenqing, born at the end of the 20th century, a native of Sichuan who grew up in Xinjiang. I once aspired to engage in legal affairs, and I have worked in Sichuan, Fujian, and Jiangsu. I also started a business in Shenzhen, Guangdong. From 2020 to 2023, the atmosphere around me took a sharp turn for the worse, and I deeply felt that ordinary people like me were in a precarious situation. I then decided to study Computer Science at Illinois Wesleyan University.

期待与你分享我在北美大陆上新的冒险。

I look forward to sharing my new adventures in North America with you.
